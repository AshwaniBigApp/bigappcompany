About the client

Digitask

Digitask is a marketplace for local services. They help customers hire trusted professionals for all their service needs. They are staffed with young, passionate people working tirelessly to make a difference in the lives of people by catering to their service needs at their doorsteps. They provide housekeeping services which consist of Plumbers, Electricians, Carpenters, Cleaning and Pest Control. They also provide personal services like beauty, spa, mobile and other appliance repairs etc.

Founders are focused to become the number one service provider in Australia. BigAppCompany is working very closely with their team to build end to end technology from prototyping to launch.
